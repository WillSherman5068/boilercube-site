BoilerCube is excited to welcome **Dr. Gabriella Nagy** as a new faculty advisor to the team.

Dr. Nagy brings expertise in chemical engineering, research, and student mentorship, and will be supporting BoilerCube as the team continues to develop its modular Direct Air Capture cube. Her guidance will be invaluable as we refine our technical design, explore deployment pathways, and prepare for upcoming competitions and outreach opportunities.

As a student-led engineering organization, BoilerCube benefits greatly from faculty advisors who provide technical insight, academic perspective, and industry awareness. Dr. Nagy’s involvement strengthens our ability to pursue innovative, real-world solutions while maintaining strong engineering fundamentals.

We are grateful for Dr. Nagy’s willingness to support the team and look forward to working with her as BoilerCube continues to grow and take on new challenges.
